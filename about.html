About Orbital Lands Consultancy

Orbital Lands Consultancy was founded in the year 2025 by a group of engineers who graduated from Machakos University. The firm was established to provide affordable, reliable, and professional land consultancy services to Kenyan citizens.

We specialize in land use planning, surveying and mapping, development control, project management, and land administration liaison. Our team is committed to integrity, accuracy, and customer satisfaction.
